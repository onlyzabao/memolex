Introduction

Have you ever asked yourself why English is so important? We all know that English is considered the global language, with hundreds of countries having adopted it as their second language. Here are the reasons why:

  • The English language is the bridge that connects us to the body of knowledge of humanity

    "You live a new life for every language you speak. If you only know one language, you live only once." (French proverb)

  • Traveling everywhere is so much easier when you know English

    English is the powerful assistant that makes your trip easy, convenient, meaningful, and memorable. "The limits of my language means the limits of my world" (Ludwig Wittgenstein)

  • Learning English helps our brain to develop

    According to scientific research, people who speak more than one language often have better memory, creativity, and mental flexibility compared to those who only know one language

Featured topics

Featured topics

Greetings

Although basic, greetings are extremely important in English communication. It's not just about saying 'Hi' or 'Hello', different types of relationships require different ways of greeting. This is a step that helps the person you're talking to evaluate and form a good impression of you

Relationships

All people in society today are connected to each other through relationships: friends, family, romantic partners, strangers, etc. We need to know how to address and communicate with these relationships appropriately and accurately

Human

Learning to describe people will help you use it in many situations and contexts. As a result, your communication skills will be improved significantly. Please note this topic if you are looking to improve your basic English vocabulary

Direction

The topic is very important for those who want to learn English for travel purposes. If you want to receive directions to a certain location, you need to have a certain amount of vocabulary to ask and understand the response of the native speaker. Learning how to describe your current location is also important if you need help or rescue while abroad

Food

In this topic, you need to learn how to name some basic dishes in English. Along with that, the adjectives to describe the taste also need to be improved. In addition, English vocabulary about the ingredients that make up the dish is also needed to be learned if you want to go to the market and cook for yourself in a foreign country

Shopping

Shopping is also a topic that needs to be invested in if you want to develop your basic English language skills. It's not just about asking and bargaining for products, it's also a topic that girls love and often mention in everyday conversations. Therefore, developing vocabulary related to shopping will help you create more lively and interesting dialogues
