Introduction

Have you ever asked yourself why English is so important? We all know that English is considered the global language, with hundreds of countries having adopted it as their second language. Here are the reasons why:

  • The English language is the bridge that connects us to the body of knowledge of humanity

    "You live a new life for every language you speak. If you only know one language, you live only once." (French proverb)

  • Traveling everywhere is so much easier when you know English

    English is the powerful assistant that makes your trip easy, convenient, meaningful, and memorable. "The limits of my language means the limits of my world" (Ludwig Wittgenstein)

  • Learning English helps our brain to develop

    According to scientific research, people who speak more than one language often have better memory, creativity, and mental flexibility compared to those who only know one language
